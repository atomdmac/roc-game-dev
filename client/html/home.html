Who We Are

ROC Game Dev is a collective of video game developers, artists and enthusiasts based in Rochester NY USA. Membership is free to anyone who wants to develop or discuss video games.

Get Involved!

  • Come to a Workshop meet-up, held on the first Wednesday of every month. These are structured events where a member of our community presents on a game development-related topic. Come participate in person or remotely from Twitch.
  • Attend a Social meet-up. These are informal gatherings that are held monthly on every third Saturday.
  • Help improve this website by forking it on GitHub and submitting a pull request.
